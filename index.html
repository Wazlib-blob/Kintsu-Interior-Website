About Kintsu Interior

Award-Winning Interior Design Studio in Singapore

Founded on the philosophy of kintsugi — the Japanese art of repairing with gold — Kintsu Interior transforms spaces into living expressions of character and care. We believe every home carries a story, and our role is to help that story shine.

From Japandi tranquillity to Scandinavian warmth, our team of dedicated designers works hand-in-hand with each client to craft spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful.

How We Work

Our Process

01
Consultation

We begin with a no-obligation call to understand your vision, lifestyle, and budget — and explore what's possible for your home.

02
Concept Design

Your designer presents moodboards, layout plans, and material palettes tailored to your style and space constraints.

03
Renovation

We manage all trades, timelines, and site visits. You receive regular updates — we handle every detail on the ground.

04
Handover

We walk you through every finish before handing over your keys. Our aftercare commitment doesn't stop at handover.
